Fiskars Phone and Garden Pocket

It may be the dead of winter, but why not start passing the time by planning for warmer weather and springtime pursuits? The Fiskars Phone and Garden Pocket is a great new gardening accessory that you can start playing with now, even before the snow melts. With it’s snazzy multi-pocket design and adjustable belt, the Fiskars Phone and Garden Pocket can provide you with the perfect excuse to dust off your gardening toys and play a little. You will be both surprised and impressed by how much the pockets can carry: trowels, shears, gloves, pruners – the Fiskars Phone and Garden Pocket turns a run-of-the-mill planting enthusiast into a superhero gardener.
